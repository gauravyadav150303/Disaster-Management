Government Support for Natural Disaster Victims

Together We Rebuild, Recover, and Rise

Learn about government programs designed to help communities affected by natural disasters.

Overview

The government offers a wide range of initiatives to assist citizens during natural disasters. These programs provide financial aid, medical services, temporary housing, and support for rebuilding livelihoods and infrastructure.

Support Programs

Disaster Relief Fund

Immediate cash support for victims to cover urgent needs after a disaster.

Emergency Shelter

Temporary housing facilities for displaced families and individuals.

Livelihood Restoration

Financial help and resources to resume employment or small businesses.

Healthcare Assistance

Medical camps, medicines, and treatment for those affected by calamities.

FAQs

How can I apply for aid?

You can apply through the official disaster management portal or visit your nearest government office with proper documents.

Who is eligible for relief?

All individuals and families directly impacted by the disaster are eligible for government support.

Is there a helpline?

Yes. National Disaster Helpline: 1078 (Toll-Free)
